# Roadmap

The maintained roadmap lives at the repository root: [ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md).

Summary of the guiding rule: data integrity, portability and offline reliability are
never sacrificed to ship roadmap features. Planned areas include community plugins and
themes (built on the existing registries), optional end-to-end-encrypted sync, a deeper
Zotero integration, canvas, publish-to-web, and strictly on-device AI integrations —
all opt-in, all consistent with [privacy.md](privacy.md).
